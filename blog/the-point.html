First of all, hello! This is meant to be the introductory post for everything that I will write on here.

I feel deeply saddened by the state of mathematics in the United States. Of course, it is only natural that not everyone will enjoy math to the same degree, as would be the case with any subject. However, I feel saddened by the fact that outside of a relatively small sect of people, math almost universally hated. It is my belief that most people do not actually know what math is, and that the system which teaches people math is fundamentally flawed.
